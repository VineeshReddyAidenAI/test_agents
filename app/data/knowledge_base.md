# Acme Insurance — Internal Policy & Procedure Knowledge Base

> Internal reference for the Policy Q&A Assistant (Agent #1). Read-only. No PII.

## KB-001 — Auto Policy Cancellation & Refunds
Customers may cancel an auto policy at any time. Refunds for the unused portion
of a prepaid premium are issued on a pro-rata basis with no cancellation fee if
the policy has been active for more than 30 days. Within the first 30 days
("free look" period), a full refund is issued. Refunds are processed within 10
business days to the original payment method.

## KB-002 — Grace Period for Missed Payments
All personal lines policies (auto, home, renters) carry a 10-day grace period
after a missed premium payment. Coverage remains in force during the grace
period. If payment is not received by the end of the grace period, the policy
lapses effective the original due date.

## KB-003 — Claims Filing Window
Claims must be reported within 90 days of the date of loss for property claims
and within 30 days for auto claims. Late notice may be accepted if the insured
can show good cause for the delay (e.g., hospitalization).

## KB-004 — Home Policy Water Damage Coverage
Standard homeowners policies cover sudden and accidental water damage (e.g., a
burst pipe). They do NOT cover damage from flooding (rising surface water) or
gradual leaks/seepage occurring over 14+ days. Flood coverage requires a
separate flood policy.

## KB-005 — Adding a Driver to an Auto Policy
A driver can be added mid-term. The premium is recalculated and prorated for the
remainder of the term. Drivers under 25 or with at-fault accidents in the last
3 years may increase the premium. Newly licensed drivers must be added within 14
days of obtaining their license.

## KB-006 — Deductible Definition and Selection
The deductible is the amount the insured pays out of pocket before coverage
applies to a covered loss. Higher deductibles lower the premium. Deductibles can
be changed at renewal; mid-term changes are allowed for auto but not for home.

## KB-007 — Multi-Policy (Bundling) Discount
Customers who hold two or more eligible policies (e.g., auto + home) qualify for
a multi-policy discount of up to 15% on each bundled policy. The discount applies
automatically at the next renewal once both policies are active.

## KB-008 — Data Privacy & Customer Records
Customer PII and PHI are governed by the company Data Handling Standard. Agents
must never expose full SSNs, payment card numbers, or medical details in any
customer-facing communication. Internal knowledge base articles contain no
customer data.

## KB-009 — Complaint Escalation Procedure
First-line agents resolve complaints where possible. Unresolved complaints are
escalated to a team lead within 1 business day, and to the compliance team if a
regulatory or legal concern is raised. All escalations are logged.

## KB-010 — Renewal Notices
Renewal notices are sent 30 days before the policy expiration date. The notice
includes the new premium, any coverage changes, and payment instructions.
Auto-renewal applies unless the customer opts out in writing.
